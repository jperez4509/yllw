JOEL PEREZ / BIO

B.A. Mass Communication and Journalism

Joel Perez was awarded the Outstanding Advertising Graduate award by California State University, Fresno in 2011. While working on his undergrad Joel was the Creative Director for the NSAC JC Penney Campaign where his team placed 3rd in regionals and was also the National Sales Manager for the The Collegian, the award winning newspaper at Fresno State.


Since graduation he has used his educational background to design and write employee training manuals for California LifeLine and Covered CA. His attention to detail and ability to create beautiful design has been appreciated and admired by all of his past and current employers.


On his free time Joel volunteers as Event Coordinator for Critical Mass Fresno. He founded Critical Mass Fresno with a group of cycling advocates after seeing a community need for a family friendly community bike ride that was open to both beginning and advanced cyclist. Critical Mass Fresno has now grown to over 100 cyclist each ride. He also has a passion for art and likes attending Art Hop on a monthly basis and supporting local artist and local businesses in the Fresno area.
